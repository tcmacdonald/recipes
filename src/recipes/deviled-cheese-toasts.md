ingredients:
  - 8 ounces grated sharp cheddar
  - 1/4 cup chopped dill pickles
  - 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  - 2 tablespoons chopped pimientos
  - Kosher salt
  - Hot sauce
---

* Mix ingredients together
* Spread on 4 slices toasted country-style bread and broil until melted, about 4 minutes
